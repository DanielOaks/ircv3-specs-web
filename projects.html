Projects

Atheme is the community behind many innovative open-source software products like the Atheme IRC platform, as well as the organization responsible for research & development efforts such as the IRCv3 working group. You can find a full list of our current projects and research on this page.


Atheme IRC Platform

Atheme Services are our flagship product and the backbone of the Atheme IRC Platform. They are used by some of the largest IRC networks in the world and have been proven to be fast and secure.

More Info

IRCv3 Group

Atheme.org is responsible for incubating development on the IRCv3 specification, and hold regular online meetings where developers from all relevant projects can contribute to the draft specification.

More Info


libmowgli

libmowgli-2 is a collection of useful frameworks and utilities for developing network-oriented C and C++ software, aiming to make developers' lives easier.

More Info

charybdis

charybdis is a well tested and stable IRC daemon that is also the IRCv3 reference implementation. charybdis is widely used by IRC networks both large and small.

More Info

tethys

tethys is our next generation IRC daemon, built to integrate better with the Atheme platform, and encourage future growth of the IRC protocol.

More Info

libguess

libguess is a high-speed character encoding detection library that can be easily linked into your C/C++ development projects to make your life much easier.
